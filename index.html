Challenge: Optimize FIFA World Cup Group-Stage Schedule

We have a schedule solved by a Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) model optimized for travel efficiency. You can build your own schedule by dragging and dropping matches below to see if you can beat the algorithm or create a better flow, while following the rules.

  • 1. Unique Slot: Each match assigned exactly one stadium & day.
  • 2. Daily Rhythm: 4 matches/day (Rounds 1-2), 6 (Round 3).
  • 3. Venue Load: Stadiums must host a balanced number of matches.
  • 4. Partner Sync: Paired group matches must be played on the same day.
  • 5. Stadium Rest: No consecutive matches at a stadium (min 1-2 days).
  • 6. Team Rest: Min 3 full rest days between a team's matches.
